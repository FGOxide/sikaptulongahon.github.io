W H Y ?

The Philippine Red Cross (PRC) is known for its life-saving blood donation drives and unwavering support during typhoons and natural disasters, providing relief, rescue, and medical assistance. Despite their critical efforts, their contributions are often overlooked. This community outreach project recognizes their dedication by showing appreciation through the distribution of appreciation gifts to PRC volunteers specifically in Marikina Chapter.

Our program entitled "Sikap, Tulong, Ahon" is dedicated to expressing gratitude to the selfless volunteers of the Philippine Red Cross Marikina Chapter by providing them with appreciation gifts. It’s our way of recognizing their invaluable service and inspiring continued commitment to humanitarian work.
