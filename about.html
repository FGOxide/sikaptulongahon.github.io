ABOUT OUR PROJECT

The Philippine Red Cross (PRC) plays a vital role in saving lives through blood donation drives, rescue operations, and providing relief during disasters. Despite their tireless efforts, PRC volunteers often go unrecognized for their dedication. Our program, "Sikap, Tulong, Ahon," aims to honor the selfless volunteers of the PRC Marikina Chapter by distributing appreciation gifts. Through this initiative, we hope to recognize their invaluable service and inspire continued commitment to humanitarian work.

"HONORING SELFLESS HEROES, INSPIRING GRATITUDE"

Our goal is to honor the dedication of Philippine Red Cross Marikina Chapter volunteers by showing gratitude for their selfless service and inspiring others to support humanitarian efforts.
