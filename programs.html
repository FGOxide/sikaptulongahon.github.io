Our program, "Sikap, Tulong, Ahon," is dedicated to expressing gratitude to the selfless volunteers of the Philippine Red Cross Marikina Chapter. Through this initiative, we provide appreciation gifts as a way to recognize their invaluable contributions to the community. By doing so, we aim to inspire continued commitment to humanitarian work and encourage others to contribute to the cause.

Program Goals

The primary goal of "Sikap, Tulong, Ahon" is to recognize and appreciate the tireless work of the volunteers from the Philippine Red Cross Marikina Chapter. Through the distribution of appreciation gifts, we aim to uplift the spirits of these volunteers and show them that their work is truly valued. Additionally, we hope to raise awareness about the vital role that the Philippine Red Cross plays in disaster response, blood donation drives, and humanitarian efforts.

How Our Programs Helps People

Through the "Sikap, Tulong, Ahon" program, we aim to create a ripple effect of positivity and compassion. By recognizing the efforts of our volunteers, we are not only boosting their morale but also strengthening the entire community. When volunteers feel appreciated, they are more motivated to continue helping others, which in turn improves the well-being of the people they serve. Additionally, our program helps by raising awareness about the importance of volunteer work and encouraging more individuals to join the cause. As more volunteers participate, we are able to reach and support more people in need, creating a more resilient and compassionate society.
